THOMAS PARTEY ON THE VERGE OF JOINING ARSENAL

According to L'Equipe, the elegant Athletico madrid midfielder Thomas Partey is reported to be on his way to the Emirate staduim

The Ghanaian has long be linked with the gunners. Prior to the end of the season, Mikel Arteta already hinted they were going for a

defensive midfielder. The likes of Ndidi, Rice, Rodrigo were all on Arsenal's radar but Partey was the man that won the heart of the

Arsenal hierarchy.

The deal is expected to be completed in the forthcoming days.
